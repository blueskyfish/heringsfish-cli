
Display help for "clean"

Description:
  Executes the maven goal "clean" in order to clean the deployment directories.

Usage:
  $ hf clean [[-v | --verbose] | [-q | --quit]]

Additional Arguments:
  -

Example:
  $ hf clean
  $ hf clean --verbose
  $ hf clean -q

Note:
  -v | --verbose       display more information.
  -q | --quiet         display only errors or warnings
